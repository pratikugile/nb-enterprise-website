Engineering Excellence for Tube Bending

At NB Enterprises, we specialize in the design and manufacturing of high-precision tube bending tooling. Our Core Products

[Image: Tube Bending Die]

Tube Bending Die

The primary forming tool that determines the bend radius. Precision-machined for exact tube outer diameters.

[Image: Mandrel assembly]

Mandrel

Internal tube support to prevent collapsing or wrinkling during tight-radius bending operations.

[Image: Clamping Die]

Clamping Die

Secures the tube against the bend die without slipping, ensuring accurate and repeatable bends.

[Image: Wiper Shoe]

Wiper Shoe

Fits precisely into the groove of the bend die to prevent wrinkling on the inside radius of the bend.

[Image: Pressure Die]

Pressure Die

Follows the tube during the bending process to provide continuous support and contain radial forces.

[Image: Collet/Collet Pad]

Collet

Precision grips used to securely hold the tube in the carriage chuck during CNC bending.

[Image: Bend Die Insert]

Bend Die Insert

Replaceable wear parts for the clamp section of the bend die, extending overall tool life.
