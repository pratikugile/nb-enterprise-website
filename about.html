About Us

Delivering precision and reliability in tube bending tooling for over a decade.

Our Story

Founded on the principles of engineering excellence and rigorous quality standards, NB Enterprises has grown to become a leading name in the manufacturing of precision tube bending tooling in India.

We began our journey with a singular focus: to solve the complex bending challenges faced by industries ranging from automotive to aerospace. Today, our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility houses advanced CNC machinery and a dedicated team of metallurgical experts and machinists.

Every die, mandrel, and wiper shoe that leaves our floor is a testament to our commitment to precision. We don't just manufacture tools; we engineer solutions that improve your operational efficiency and end-product quality.

We also undertake Plastic Injection Moulds, Rubber Moulds PDC, GDC Dies, Jigs & Fixture, and all types of Precision Job Works.
